Beer Run

Est. 2018

Beer Run is an app that takes the stuggle out of choosing who is the designated driver or the "beer run guy", so everyone can enjoy the party uninturrupted!

If you want to help keep the party going for our awesome patrons, help prevent drunk driving and make up to $20/per hour, then click the link below to apply!


All you need to participate is:


  • A valid Driver's Liscense
  • A bank account or paypal account
  • A safe, reliable vehicle (make and model doesn't matter)
  • Availability on evenings and weekend
  • An Android or Apple Smart phone
